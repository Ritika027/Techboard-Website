About

The Technical Board of our college is a dynamic platform that fosters innovation and technical excellence. It organizes a wide range of events, including hackathons, coding contests, workshops, and technical fests, offering students opportunities to enhance their skills and showcase their talents. Through collaborations with industry professionals and hosting guest lectures, it bridges the gap between academia and real-world applications. The board also encourages students to participate in inter-college competitions, promoting a culture of learning, collaboration, and problem-solving.
